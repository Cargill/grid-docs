Hyperledger Grid

Hyperledger Grid is a framework for building
distributed supply chain solutions.

Grid provides implementations of open, standards-based business processes and data concepts that immediately align your trading relationships and accelerate your shared time to value.

Why Grid?

  • Prebuilt content: Grid provides supply-chain-centric smart contracts, data models, and more, with out-of-the-box support for GS1 standards.
  • Flexible deployment models: Combine a distributed ledger framework with Hyperledger components and Grid smart contracts into a single, effective business solution.
  • Open and free: Grid is an open source project on GitHub.
